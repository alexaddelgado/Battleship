BATTLESHIP

Battleship is a game based on luck and strategy where the player has to sink all of their opponent's ships before their opponent sinks their own.

The rules for this game are:

      1. Place your ships in their desired locations without overlapping any of them.

      2. Press start when you are ready to play.

      3. Select a block in the opponent's grid to attack. If you get a hit or miss, the information display in between the boards will let you know.

      4. Wait for the opponent to attack. Turn will be indicated in between the two boards.

      5. First player to sink all of the opposing side's ships will win!

      6. To play again, select "New Game".

Note: When a ship is sunk, its location will turn black.
